Welcome to HMN College

Smt. Hiraben Manilal Nanavati Junior College Of Home Science With Science started in the year 1989.The past couple of years have seen a resurging of interest in the Home Science course. Students have realized the variety of opportunities the course offers. The Junior College of Home Science is no longer a limitation of a specialized course, but rather it gives the students many more options to pursue their career.

FROM THE PRINCIPAL'S DESK

Dear Friends,

Greetings!

The academic year 2023-2024 marked the implementation of the National Education Policy (NEP 2020). Our college is among the first SNDTWU colleges to begin instruction as per the new system of higher education and we now offer several innovative learning options as per the policy. In addition to academic progression, faculty and students continued to bring laurels to the college in a variety of co-curricular activities. Students of the Department of Computer Applications participated in a co-design coding project with Wilkes University, Pennsylvania, USA. We also hosted a collaborative workshop (online) on ‘Mentoring’ in collaboration with the faculty of Wilkes University in April 2025. Another international presence was visiting scholar Dr. Delphine Munos from the University of Liege, Belgium, under a National Fund for Scientific Research (FNRS) grant.
